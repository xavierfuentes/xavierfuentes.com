# Content Pillar Definitions

## Pillar Distribution Targets

| Pillar | Target | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| `technology-strategy` | 30% | Technical architecture, build-vs-buy, tech stack decisions, technical debt |
| `leadership-management` | 25% | Engineering leadership, team building, culture, hiring, 1:1s |
| `execution-delivery` | 20% | Shipping, estimation, agile, processes, delivery patterns |
| `founder-lessons` | 15% | Personal stories, mistakes, lessons learned, founder journey |
| `market-ai-trends` | 10% | Industry trends, AI developments, market observations |

## Pillar Details

### Technology Strategy (30%)
Content about technical decision-making at the strategic level:
- Architecture patterns and trade-offs
- Build vs buy decisions
- Technical debt management
- Platform choices and migrations
- Security and compliance strategy
- Data strategy and governance

**Target audience:** CTOs, technical founders, senior engineers
**Typical outcomes:** inbound_leads, authority

### Leadership & Management (25%)
Content about leading engineering teams:
- Hiring and team building
- Performance management
- Engineering culture
- Communication patterns
- Career development
- Stakeholder management

**Target audience:** Engineering managers, directors, VPs
**Typical outcomes:** authority, pipeline_warm

### Execution & Delivery (20%)
Content about shipping effectively:
- Estimation and planning
- Agile/process patterns
- Release management
- Technical project management
- Quality assurance strategies
- Cross-functional collaboration

**Target audience:** Tech leads, engineering managers, product managers
**Typical outcomes:** authority, newsletter_signup

### Founder Lessons (15%)
Personal stories and experiences:
- Mistakes and lessons learned
- Behind-the-scenes insights
- Founder journey moments
- Contrarian takes from experience
- Vulnerability and authenticity

**Target audience:** Founders, aspiring CTOs
**Typical outcomes:** authority, pipeline_warm

### Market & AI Trends (10%)
Industry observations and trends:
- AI/ML developments and implications
- Industry shifts and patterns
- Market observations
- Technology predictions
- Commentary on industry news

**Target audience:** All personas
**Typical outcomes:** authority, newsletter_signup
