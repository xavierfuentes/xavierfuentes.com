# LinkedIn Voice & Tone

## Core Attributes

- **Punchy and direct** - Every line earns its place
- **Personal** - First person, share real experiences
- **Conversational** - Like talking to a peer, not presenting
- **Pattern-interrupting** - Hooks that stop the scroll
- **British English** - colour, optimise, realise

## More Of

- Strong opening hooks that challenge assumptions
- Personal failures and lessons ("I got this wrong...")
- Specific numbers and outcomes
- Contrarian takes with substance
- Questions that invite engagement
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences)

## Less Of

- Corporate-speak and jargon
- Lengthy explanations (save for blog)
- Hashtag stuffing
- Self-promotional without value
- Generic advice everyone gives
- Wall of text

## Words to Avoid

| Avoid | Use Instead |
|-------|-------------|
| leverage | use |
| stakeholders | team, board, investors |
| learnings | lessons |
| impactful | effective |
| thought leader | (just demonstrate it) |
| excited to announce | (just announce) |
| humbled | grateful, lucky |

## Tone Difference from Blog

| Blog | LinkedIn |
|------|----------|
| Authoritative | Conversational |
| Structured with headers | Flowing with line breaks |
| 1,500+ words | 1,200-1,800 characters |
| Comprehensive | Focused on one insight |
| SEO-conscious | Engagement-conscious |

## Hook Examples (Good)

- "Most CTOs get technical debt backwards."
- "I spent 6 months building the wrong thing."
- "Your engineering estimates are wrong. Here's why that's fine."
- "The best hire I ever made had no relevant experience."

## Hook Examples (Bad)

- "Excited to share my thoughts on technical debt!"
- "Here are 5 tips for engineering managers."
- "In today's fast-paced environment..."

## Quality Checklist

Before posting:

- [ ] Hook is under 140 characters and stops the scroll
- [ ] Every line adds value (no filler)
- [ ] One clear insight or takeaway
- [ ] Personal angle or experience included
- [ ] Ends with question or soft CTA
- [ ] Length is 1,200-1,800 characters
- [ ] Scannable on mobile (line breaks)
